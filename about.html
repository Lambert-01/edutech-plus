About Us

Welcome to Edutech+

EduTech+ is an innovative educational platform dedicated to transforming learning and teaching experiences in Rwanda. We leverage advanced technology to deliver a wide range of courses, track student performance, and facilitate remote learning.

Our platform offers guidance and support from skilled instructors, interactive virtual classrooms for peer collaboration, and a variety of educational tools. Our dedicated team provides personalized student support, ensuring learners receive guidance, answers to queries, and assistance throughout their educational journey, fostering academic and personal growth.
